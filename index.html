Features

AI Portraits

The AI Portraits feature uses advanced AI technology to create personalized avatars that look just like you. Simply upload a photo of yourself and the app will generate an AI portrait that is remarkably similar to the real you. You can then customize your AI portrait with a variety of hairstyles, facial features, and clothing options..

User-friendly interface

The app's simple and intuitive interface makes it easy for users of all ages to create unique and personalized avatars without any prior design experience.

Printing

Once you have created your AI portraits, you can print it on a variety of real-world products, including t-shirts, mugs, jewelry, and more. The printing process is easy and convenient, and the quality of the printing is amazing.

Sharing

Once you have printed your AI portrait, you can share it with friends and family on social media. Simply take a photo of your printed product and upload it to your social media account. Your friends and family will be able to see your AI portrait and purchase their own prints.

Frequently asked questions

  • How much does PrintMyAI cost?
    Pricing starts from as little as $7.95 to get your first set of avatars. Please be informed that this is a temporary price and it will likely to change in the future.
  • How long will it take to get my avatars?
    On average, it takes 10-30 mins to get your AI portraits. However, sometimes due to high load, it can take up to 1-2 hours. We'll notify you through the push notification in the app when your portraits are ready.
  • How much will my AI portraits look like me?
    Prepare to be amazed by how much these AI portraits look like you. It's like I took a snapshot of your face and painted it with different styles (don't worry, I didn't). But don't expect perfection from me. I'm just an AI and sometimes I make mistakes (like giving you extra limbs or digits). That's why I generate a bunch of them (4-8 per style) so you can pick the ones that don't make you look like a mutant.
  • What happens to the photos I upload on the app?
    Any photos that you upload to the app are deleted from our servers right after we finish generating your AI portraits. Any images generated by the app, such as AI portraits, are deleted from our servers after 30 days.
  • Is there a free trial of PrintMyAI?
    Hey there, fellow human! I know you're probably wondering why we don't have a free trial or demo version of our AI portraits generator. Well, it's simple: it costs a lot of computational resources to generate AI portraits, and even one generated avatar costs us money. That's why we're unfortunately not able to provide a service for free.
